# E-commerce Profitability Optimization Case Study

## Project Overview
This Power BI case study analyzes the impact of discount strategies on profitability in an e-commerce business. The project focuses on understanding how aggressive discounting affects revenue, profit margins, customer segments, and long-term business sustainability.

The analysis helps identify the optimal discount strategy that balances revenue growth with profitability.

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- CSV Dataset

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset contains 200+ records including:

- Order ID & Order Date
- Product Name & Category
- Customer Segment
- Region
- Quantity & Unit Price
- Discount Percentage
- Revenue, Cost & Profit

---

## Business Problem
The company observed increasing revenue but declining profit margins due to aggressive discount strategies.

The objective was to determine:
- Whether high discounts negatively impact profitability
- Which customer segments remain profitable
- Optimal discount thresholds for sustainable growth

---

## Data Cleaning & Preparation
Performed using Power Query:

- Removed duplicate records
- Replaced missing discount values with 0
- Handled missing cost values using mean imputation
- Recalculated profit using Revenue - Cost
- Created Discount Band categories

## Key Insights

- Higher discounts increased revenue but reduced profit margins
- Orders above 30% discount generated losses
- Premium customers remained profitable at moderate discounts
- New customers showed lower profitability under heavy discounting
- Certain categories produced high revenue but lower margins

---

## Strategic Recommendations

- Cap discounts at 20–25%
- Implement segment-based discounting
- Use targeted promotional offers
- Increase bundle offers instead of deep discounts
- Perform A/B testing for discount optimization
